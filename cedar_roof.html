Cedar roofing

You Don’t Have to Worry About Molds Anymore

A Cedar roof is one of the most durable roofs available today. It has a natural resistance to mold, mildew, and decay. It also has a high load-bearing capacity that allows it to withstand weight from snow and ice without developing sags or lifting problems. Moreover, cedar is strong enough to be exposed to harsh weather conditions. It's also resistant to decay, rotting wood, and insect infestation because it's naturally resistant to those things.

Why Should You Get Cedar Roofing Services?

It's easy to recognize when your cedar shingle roof has sustained damage because it will be discolored in either a pattern or an area where there is no color. To determine if your cedar shingle roof is damaged, check for discoloration that resembles a pattern that corresponds to nails. Also watch out for discoloration in an area where there are no nails, which indicates straight-line damage. Straight-line damage can be caused by squirrels, ice, or hail.
