Stucco removal

Get Rid of Stucco from Your Walls Today Without Damaging Your Walls

When stucco is removed from an exterior wall, it can cause problems for the owner. Even if the owner is not aware of these issues, they will want to have a qualified professional remove the stucco so that no further damage can be done. The benefits include improved structural integrity of the building and potential energy savings in the form of better insulation with a more tightly sealed building envelope. In addition, there are aesthetic gains in terms of cleaner walls and a cleaner overall look for both outside and inside.

Benefits of Stucco Removal

Stucco removal has its risks but these risks can be minimized through proper preparation and also by avoiding faulty installation methods that contributed to the need for removal in the first place. For example, exterior walls with a history of leaks will be more difficult to work on than new construction. If the stucco has been applied over a soft substrate like plywood, the stucco may be a secondary layer that will not provide structural support for the building and may even be damaged further by water infiltration problems that existed before the stucco was applied. Hence, it’s important to hire professionals who know their job well.

There are several factors to consider when planning for a stucco removal project. The most important factor is proper preparation of the building and wall surface. This includes repairing and/or replacing any elements of a defective roof drainage system such as gutters, downspouts, and flashing membranes. To ensure that everything is perfect, make sure you hire professionals for the job.
